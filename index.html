About ABG

Anonymous by GEA was created as a way to express art and design in its purest form. A perfect blend of the arts and the sciences. ABG merges art with functionality, giving way to practicality and reminding us of reality.

"To create art is to fully exist in all of humanity. The unknown should be embraced and not feared. After all, mysteries are the fuel to a life well lived."

MEET GEA

GEA embraces both the arts and the sciences.She is an advocate of education, equality, peace, and health. She has a bachelor of science in biomedical sciences with a minor in mathematics and she is currently a Master of Public Health Candidate with a concentration in Community Health. She enjoys creating and innovating, traveling, planning events, being a foodie and the art of fashion. GEA also enjoys learning about and embracing different cultures, languages, and a multitude of indoor activities.
